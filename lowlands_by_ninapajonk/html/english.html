ABOUT A CAMPINGFLIGHT TO LOWLANDS PARADISE

When and where is Lowlands 2019?

Lowlands takes place on August 16 + 17 + 18, 2019 at Evenemententerrein Walibi Holland at Biddinghuizen, The Netherlands. Click here to find our location on Google Maps. You can arrive at Thursday August 15 from 9:00 AM in the morning and leave at Monday August 19 at noon.

What is Lowlands?

A Campingflight To Lowlands Paradise is exactly what it promises to be: a true backpacker’s Garden Of Eden and Holland’s most adventurous cultural outdoorevent, offering a cutting edge choice of only the best in alternative music, film, stand-up comedy, visual arts, literature and street theatre. For three days in August, a township with 60,000 inhabitants arises in the middle of the country. The Lowlands festival hosts no less than twelve stages in three large areas (among which a cinema and theatre), about 250 different acts and performances, dozens of restaurants from all corners of the earth, food and drink stalls, a large market with everything from clothes and CD stores to a barber’s shop, relax rooms with brain massages, sports facilities, a sauna / hottub area, its own currency, a local radio station, a daily newspaper (Dutch only) and, last but not least: seven luxury campsites with hot showers and real, flushable toilets. A Campingflight To Lowlands Paradise really is the ultimate summer holiday extravaganza!

A lot of music...

Though Lowlands covers a wide spectrum of different cultural activities, the main focus still is on alternative music: from rock to pop via hip hop; from metal to electro via world music and hereby not neglecting drum ‘n bass, house and techno. Acts announced for Lowlands 2019 so far are Tame Impala, Twenty One Pilots, A$AP Rocky, Anderson .Paak & The Free Nationals, The National, New Order, Royal Blood and many more. Watch this page for all acts announced so far.

General ticket info

Admission tickets for Lowlands 2019 are €210 (€10,- Ticketmaster fee included) and tickets are only available through Ticketmaster Netherlands. Access to the regular camping and the use of our shuttle bus service is always included in the price of a ticket.

If you would like to bring a camping vehicle, please buy an additional ticket for our Camper Camping. Pre-pitched tents, tipi's, flexotels and other accomodations are available through Camp Solutions.

Day tickets?

Lowlands is a three day festival so no day tickets are sold. If you would like to exit and later re-enter the festival: your wristband is valid for the whole weekend and you can access Lowlands 24/7, but please note that you can only bring in drinks (alcohol and non-alcohol) the first time you enter the campsite with a maximum of 8 liters per person.

Where to buy tickets

GETTING THERE

By car?

Navigate to Bijsselseweg 31, Biddinghuizen, The Netherlands and follow the signs Walibi Holland and P Lowlands. By following the signs, you will get directed to the nearest available parking location. Please note that cars are banned on the camp site. Illegally parked vehicles will be towed away. Please don't forget to buy an additional parking ticket via Ticketmaster. Presale parking tickets are €15, - (Ticketmaster fee included). Did you forget to buy a parking ticket in advance? It will cost you €25,- on site. You can only pay by debit card on site, cash is not accepted. Your parking ticket is valid for the entire weekend.

By airplane?

Are you flying to Lowlands? Our festival site is only one hour away from Amsterdam Schiphol Airport and only two hours away from Rotterdam - The Hague Airport and Eindhoven Airport. It's easy getting from these airports to Lowlands: you can use The Netherlands's reliable public transport network to travel to Lelystad train station. From here, you can hop on a free shuttle bus which will bring you to Lowlands in about twenty minutes. Planning your public transport journey? The 9292OV public transport journey planner or the NS train journey planner might come in handy. You can buy your public transport tickets at the tram, train, metro or bus station where you're departing from: it's not neccessary to buy your tickets in advance. Please see the shuttle bus schedule under 'Shuttle bus service'.

By train?

It's easy to get to Lowlands by train: please navigate to Lelystad train station. Lowlands is only an hour away from Amsterdam Central Station. From Lelystad, you can take a free shuttle bus to Lowlands. Planning your public transport journey? The 9292OV public transport journey planner or the NS train journey planner might come in handy. You can buy your public transport tickets at the tram, train, metro or bus station where you're departing from: it's not neccessary to buy your tickets in advance. Travelling from London? It is now possible to take the super-fast Eurostar train, which brings you to Amsterdam in less than four hours. Get the tickets for the Eurostar train here. From Amsterdam Central Station you can travel to Lelystad. Take the Lowlands shuttle bus from there.

Shuttle bus service

We offer a completely free shuttle bus service from Lelystad Train Station and Dronten Train Station to Lowlands. Please note: do you want to catch the last bus of the day from Lowlands to one of both train stations? Then keep in mind that you'll have to leave the festival before the headlining show ends.

By coach?

It's possible to travel to Lowlands by coach. Coaches depart from more than 500 locations in The Netherlands. Please visit our partner Event Travel's website for more information and tickets.

By bike?

You can park your bike at our bicycle parking area. Just follow the signs.

By motorcycle?

Are you bringing your motorcycle? You can park your motorcycle at our Camper Camping: you can use one Camper Camping ticket 2 motorbikes + 1 tent tents OR 2 + 1 engine. You can also park your motorbike at our motorcycle parking area (near the entrance) and stay at our regular camping. Please note you'll need an additional Camper Camping ticket if you would like to camp on our Camper Camping.

By camping vehicle?

You're welcome to bring your own RV, trailer or tent trailer, but please note that you'll have to buy an additional Camper Camping ticket, which costs you €89 (Ticketmaster fee included) for 1 camping vehicle without electricity connection and €169 (Ticketmaster fee included) for a ticket for 1 vehicle with electricity connection included. Only camping vehicles that meet the requirements laid down in the Dutch Road Traffic Regulations are allowed. Buses and vans are not allowed. Is your vehicle longer than 8,5m? Please email info@lowlands.nl for additional rules and the entry form. Gas cylinders are only allowed in RV's and not trailers and trailer tents and only when a recent MOT report (max.1 year old) can be shown. Are you bringing a trailer or tent trailer? You don't have to buy an additional parking ticket: this is included in the price of your Camper Camping ticket. To reach our Camper Camping, please navigate to Alikruikweg 23, Biddinghuizen, The Netherlands and follow the signs to 'Camper Camping' as soon as you see them.
